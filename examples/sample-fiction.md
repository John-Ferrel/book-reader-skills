# The Lantern Street

Mara waited at the corner where the old lantern flickered even at noon.

## Scene: the returned key

When Ivo returned the brass key, Mara did not unlock the door. She put it in
her coat and asked why the lantern had gone dark the night he left.

## Scene: the lit window

By evening the upstairs window was lit, but no one crossed the street. The key
had become less a tool than a promise neither character wanted to name.

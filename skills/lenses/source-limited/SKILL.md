# Source-Limited Lens

Use when extraction is degraded, modalities are missing, source text is partial,
or evidence confidence is low.

Focus on what can be safely inferred, what cannot be claimed, missing
modalities, source confidence, and recovery plan.

Read `references/source-limited-reading.md`.
